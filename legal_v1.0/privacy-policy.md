## 1. Introduction

Rock Something ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Rock Something mobile application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our App.

By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

**Account Information:**
- Email address
- Password (stored in hashed form; we never have access to your plaintext password)

**Profile Information:**
- Username
- Date of birth
- Height (metric or imperial)
- Weight (metric or imperial)
- Unit system preference (metric/imperial)
- Profile photo/avatar (optional)

**User Content:**
- Climbing videos and photos you upload
- Titles and descriptions of climbing sessions
- 3D models generated from your video content

**Social Information:**
- Users you follow and users who follow you
- Follow requests you send, accept, or decline

**Settings and Preferences:**
- Profile visibility (public/private)
- Follow approval preferences
- Activity visibility settings
- Notification preferences

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

**Device Information:**
- Device type and operating system (iOS or Android)
- App version

**Location Data (with your permission):**
- GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) when you tag a climbing session
- Reverse-geocoded location information (city, region, country, street)
- You can choose whether to share location data on a per-session basis

**Usage Data:**
- Content view counts
- Processing status of uploaded videos

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services
- We do not collect browsing history or track you across other apps
- We do not collect advertising identifiers
- We do not use cookies (see our Cookies & Local Storage Policy for details)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide the Service** — create and manage your account, process your climbing videos into 3D models, and display content
- **Enable Social Features** — facilitate connections between users, process follow requests, and show profiles
- **Personalize Your Experience** — display content relevant to you and remember your preferences
- **Communicate With You** — send account-related notifications such as email verification, security alerts, and service updates
- **Maintain Security** — authenticate users, prevent fraud, and protect against unauthorized access
- **Improve the App** — understand how features are used and identify areas for improvement
- **Enforce Our Terms** — ensure compliance with our Terms of Service and address violations

## 5. Data Storage and Security

### 5.1 Where Your Data Is Stored
- **Account and profile data:** Stored on Supabase servers (cloud-hosted PostgreSQL database)
- **Media files and 3D models:** Stored on Cloudflare R2 cloud storage
- **Authentication tokens:** Stored locally on your device using encrypted secure storage (Expo Secure Store)

### 5.2 Security Measures
We implement the following measures to protect your data:
- All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
- Passwords are hashed before storage (managed by Supabase authentication)
- Authentication tokens are stored in your device's encrypted secure storage
- Database access is controlled through row-level security policies

### 5.3 No Guarantee
While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 6. Data Retention

- **Account data:** Retained for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your data within a reasonable timeframe.
- **User Content:** Videos, photos, and 3D models are retained until you delete them or close your account.
- **Location data:** Retained as part of the content metadata for as long as the associated content exists.
- **Server logs:** May be retained for a limited period for security and debugging purposes.

## 7. Your Rights and Choices

### 7.1 Account and Profile
- **Edit your information:** Update your profile, username, and physical metrics at any time through the App
- **Privacy settings:** Control your profile visibility, follow approval requirements, and activity visibility in account settings
- **Delete your account:** You can request account deletion through the App or by contacting us

### 7.2 Device Permissions
You can manage the following permissions through your device settings:
- **Camera and Microphone** — required for recording climbing videos
- **Location** — optional, used for tagging climbing sessions
- **Photo/Video Library** — required for selecting media to upload

Revoking permissions may limit certain App features.

### 7.3 Location Data
- Location access is optional and requested only when you record a climbing session
- You can choose whether to share location data on a per-session basis
- You can disable location access entirely through your device settings

- **Restrict** certain processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email address below.
